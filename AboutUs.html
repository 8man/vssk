  JURISDICTION

The jurisdiction of the trust shall be throughout India, Asia & Abroad.




 1. AIMS AND OBJECTS


 1.1 To establish, develop, maintain and grant aid in cash or in kind to hospitals, medical schools, medical colleges, nursing institutions, dispensaries, maternity homes, child welfare centres and/or such other similar charitable institutions in India for the benefit and use of the general public.


 1.2 To establish, run, support and grant aid or other financial assistance to schools, colleges, libraries, reading rooms, universities, laboratories, research and other institutions of the like nature in India, for use of the students and the staff and also for the development and advancement of education and diffusion of knowledge amongst the public in general.

.

 1.3 To establish, maintain and run studentships, scholarships and render other kind of aid to students including supply of books, stipends, medals and other incentives to study, without any distinction as to caste, colour, race, creed or sex.


 1.4. To promote, establish, support, maintain or grant aid to institutions for the promotion of science, literature, music, drama and fine arts, for the preservation of historical monuments and for the research and other institutions, in India, having similar objects for the benefit of the public in general.


 1.5 To establish, maintain or grant aid for the establishment and/or maintenance of parks, gardens, gymnasiums, sports clubs, dharamshalas and rest houses, for use by public in general.

 

1.6 To establish, maintain or grant aid to homes for the aged, orphanages or other establishments for the relief and help to the poor, needy and destitute people, orphans, widows and aged persons.


 1.7 To establish and develop institutions for the physically handicapped and disabled or mentally retarded persons and to provide them education, food, clothing or other help.


 1.8 To grant relief and assistance to the needy victims during natural calamities such as famine, earth quake, flood, fire, pestilence, etc. and to give donations and other assistance to institutions, establishments or persons engaged in such relief work.


 1.9 To construct repair and manage the marghats, cemeteries and burial grounds.

 

1.10 To grant aid or render assistance to other

 

1.11 To promote Indian and Vedic culture through various forms of Arts, Architecture, Literature, Dance, Drama, Music, Bhajans etc.

 

1.12 To research into and scientifically explore, investigate and expound the science of Ayurveda;


 1.13 To assist nations in developing universal friendly relations through science;


 1.14 To promote national integrity, enliven national consciousness, ability, adaptability and the ability to act in accordance with the laws nature, hereby restoring cultural integrity and bringing invincibility to nation.

 

1.15 To promote spontaneous integration of knowledge in daily life, awareness to the ground state of Natural law for all actions in life. Higher education for every one to fulfill the natural ability of mankind and to assist in the discovery of the fundamentals of education;


 1.16 To establish and support professorship, fellowships, Lecturer ships, Scholarship, Loan Scholarships and Prizes in any school, college, University, Vidayalaya, Vishwa Vidayalay and any other Educational Institutions.

 

 

1.17 To purchase and/ or cultivate medicinal plants, herbs etc. and prepare Ayurvedic medicines for the benefits of mankind.


 1.18 To undertake promotion and learning of Ayurvedic medicines.


 1.19 To accept any donations, gifts or other transfers to the trust of any immovable or movable properties or other assets which would assist in the implementation of or the furtherance of any of the objects and purposes of the trust or carrying out any such objects or purposes;


 1.20 To undertake, sponsor, establish, execute any trusts, society, institutions, foundations, endowments and/ or funds that may be inductive of any or all of the objects of the trust.


 1.21 To subscribe, contribute and/ or donation to any public fund or institutions established for promotion of chargeable purpose with such conditions as the trustees shall think fit;

 

1.22 To sponsor, undertake, establish and execute any trust, endowment fund for the purpose of providing relief on occasions of national calamities like earthquakes, fire, tornadoes, famines epidemics and floods or for any other purposes which may seem desired and which in law is charitable within the meaning of the provision of the income tax act, 1961.

 

1.23 To apply the funds of the trust for the advancement of any other object of general public utility;


 1.24 To undertake and promote any activity that will enable the trust's program to be self sufficient including the activity of Agriculture, Horticulture, Dairy farming, Bee farming or conducting Household, Cottage and small scale industries, khadi udyog, Architectural and constructional activities etc. with the sole motive of bringing fulfillment to be object of the trust and without any profit motive.

 

1.25 To promote balanced rural development by implementing such themes or programmes either individually or with the help of other institutions, organizations, Govt. and Semi Govt. Departments which pay benefit rural inhabitants of India.

 

1.26 To promote charitable, cultural, spiritual, educations and any other activity for the relief of poor and the education of the needy and to afford medical relief of poor and/ or promote any other object of public utility without distinction of caste, creed, religion and race.

 

1.27 To promote such activities as may result in mental and/ or physical well being of the members of the society in general.

 

1.28 To do or perform any other act which may be incidental or ancillary attainment of any of the objects of the trust.

 2.4 The number of the Trustees shall not be less than three and more than fifteen. If the number of the trustees falls below three, the Trustees shall not act, except for the purposes of filling up the vacancy, so long be number is below the said minimum.

 

2.5 The management of the affairs of the Trust and is income and properties shall be done by the Trustees, and the decision of the majority, less otherwise mentioned in these presents, shall be binding on all trustee.

 

2.6 The affairs of the trust shall be managed by the trustees who shall determine the purposes being charitable purposes within the objects of the trust as hereinbefore declared for which and the manner in which the first property shall be applied, and those decisions as to any such application of the trust within the terms of the said trust shall be final and inclusive and not open to question by any person or body or trustees.

 

2.7 The trust may apply to the Government, public bodies, urban, local, Municipal, District and other bodies, corporation, companies, or person to accept grant of money and of aid, donations, gifts, scrimptions, immovable property and other assistance with a view to note the objects of the trust and to discuss and negotiable with the Government, public and other work and matter within the objects of the trust and to confirm to any condition subjects which such grants and payments may be made.

 

2.8 The trust may take over, acquire, manage, control or aid any existing institution or institutions having objects either who or in part similar to objects of this trust and on such terms and conditions as may be sought expedient.

 

 

2.9 The " Board of trustees" may transfer/ FOUNDER TRUSTEE/SETTLER or any part of the property, movable or immovable, assets, liabilities and engagements the trust on such conditions and upon such terms as the trustees in their discretion may deem fit and proper for the purpose of the trust.

 

2.10 The Trust may borrow or raise money by way of loans or deposits in private or public appeals or banks or Financial institutions or Govts. In any manner permitted by law and to issue promissory notes, debentures, bonds or any other obligations or securities upon such terms and conditions as the Trustees shall in their discretion deem fit and proper from time to time. The trust may mortgage the whole or any part of the trust property as security for the repayment of the loans borrowed for the purposes of the trust.

 

2.11 The "Board of Trustees" may secure, hold, improve, sell, lease, convey and dispose of any property and to exercise the rights, powers and privileges of ownership.

 

2.12 The trust may apportion the trust property and to make compromise or compound all actions, suits or other proceedings and all differences and demands and refer any such differences or demands to arbitration and to adjust, settle and approve, all account relating to the trust property and to execute and release all documents necessary in the premises and to do all other things relating thereto respectively as fully as if they were absolutely entitled to the trust property and without being answerable for the loss occasioned thereby.

 

2.13 The trust may instead of themselves spending any sum for any authorized by these presents or by law or by statute the trustees any sum for such purpose to any charitable educational or Trust or Institution without being bound to see to the application thereof. Provided that every contribution by the Trustees to any other trust, or institution, having as its object all or any of the objects specified in clause 4 herein above shall be treated and deems to be in furtherance achievement of the objects of the trust.

2.18 The Membership of trustees shall be ceased in the event of His/Her death, or acceptance of resignation by the Board of Trustees. The membership can be ceased after circulating proper agenda with a cent-percent vote of the Board of trustees. In the event of death of any trustee, the new trustee shall be nominated by his/ her family members only. In the event of acceptance of registration of trustee, the vacancy shall be filled in by the Board of trustees after circulating proper agenda with a cent-percent vote of the trustees only.


2.19 The Board of Trustees can have various committees. These committees can be entrusted responsibility for running one or more institutions, However the committees will be responsible to the trust and take guidance in policy matters from the Managing trustees. In the administration and management of the trust and the trust affairs, the trustees shall be entitled to appoint such committees of persons who may be other than trustees to guide and advise the trustees or the Managing trustees as the case may be or for any other purpose whatsoever.


2.20 The Board of Trustees can appoint at the most fifteen persons as Patron/Hony/Designated trustees for the period of one year in the interest and welfare of the trust. The membership of the Patron/ Hony/ Designated trustees shall get automatically terminated at the end of one year and Patron/ Hony/ Designated trustees shall not be having any voting right in any manner whatsoever during their tenure.


2.21 There will be at least one meetings of "Board of Trustee" in a year. The agenda of the meeting would be financial status of trust construction and policy related discussion on important issues, maintenance of buildings and other movable property and on other related matters keeping in view the objectives of the trust, annual report, annual accounts and appointment of auditors etc. The trustees shall transact their business in meetings which shall be convened from time to time as and when necessary and as decided by majority of the trustees. In case of urgency any business can be transacted by a written circular to be issues by or at the instance of the Vice-President, Secretary and circulated among the trustees for recording the opinion in writing as to the matters under consideration. The opinion may also be obtained over the telephone or by way of tele-communication or on a voting by a circular as aforesaid. The vote of majority shall prevail at this meeting.


2.22 The different committees will be constituted for smooth working of trust. Trust will also form its working body consisting of trustees. Trust board can invite dignitary or dignitaries, if it feels it necessary.


2.23 The meeting of managing trustees can be called at any time i.e. as and when required but minimum one meeting in a year. It is necessary to give seven days notice for the meeting except under special circumstances, but 3 days prior information for specific general meeting would be necessary.


2.24 The Governing body will be formed for each institute. The President of the Governing body will necessarily be from among the founder trustees. One vice president, one Vice-President, Secretary, two assistant/ additional secretary, one treasurer and two members, will have to be on the Board.


2.25 In case of any conflict with any institute, run by the trust, the final decision will be of the "President". The President can remove any other t2ustee in any circumstances, if he or she is not working for the benefit of trust.


2.26 In case of any difference of opinion among the Trustees for the time being in any manner concerning the Trust including interpretation of these presents, the power of appointment of the Trustees or any other matter or question relating to or arising out of the Trust the opinion of the majority shall prevail and be binding and conclusive.

 

3. RULES AND REGULATIONS AND BYE-LAWS


The Trustees shall be entitled to make, amend or rescind rules and Regulations for the management of the trust and all matter concerning the trust and the trust property and their management including the conduct of the meetings of the trustees with the majority vote of the trustees.

     Provided that such Rules and Regulations shall not be repugnant to the provisions hereof or offend the objects or purchases of the trust or be inconsistent therewith.

 

4. ACCOUNTS & FUNDS


4.1 The trustees shall have power in their discretion to accept upon such terms as they may think fit any donations, contributions, from any private or public trust, institution, company, firm or persons whomsoever whether such donations or contributions be of money and or any other property movable or immovable.

PROVIDED ALWAYS THAT the terms upon which such donations or contributions shall be accepted, shall not in any way be inconsistent with or repugnant to the objects of these presents and shall only be for the purposes of advancement of the objects of the trust created under these presents, to be utilized only in furtherance of such objects;

PROVIDED ALWAYS AND IT IS HEREBY AGREED AND DECLARED THAT save as otherwise expressly provided by the terms of the donations or contribution as aforesaid; the trustees shall in their absolute discretion be entitled their donations or contributions or any part there of as part of trust of the trust property and to utilize such donations or contributions or any part thereof for the furtherance and fulfillment of the effects of the trust.

 

4.2 The funds and movable property of the trust shall be vested with the trustees of the trust, to be held, invested and dealt with by them as may from time to time be decided up by the trustees.


4.3 All the property and income of the trust shall be application duly towards the promotion of the objects of the trust as set forth herein above and further, that no portion thereof shall be paid or transferred directly or indirectly by way if dividend or bonus or otherwise by way of profits to the persons who at any time are or have been trustees of the trust or to any one of them or any person claiming through any of them or any person claiming through any of them. However nothing contained herein shall prevent the payment in good faith of remuneration to any employee or employees of the trust or to any trustee thereof or other person from any service rendered to the trust.

 

4.4 To keep the movable and immovable properties forming part of the trust property in good repair and condition and fully insured and in their discretion to make any addition, alteration and improvement to the immovable property/s forming part of the trust property and to develop the same from time to time in such manner as they, in their discretion deem fit and proper.


 4.5 The financial year of the trust shall commence from 1st April and close on 31st March of every year.


 4.6 The account of the Trust will be opened in any Co-operative/commercial/Nationalized/multinational/foreign bank by the trust and it shall be operated trustee among the trustees as resolved by the "Managing Trustees" in its meeting. All the bank accounts will be operated by the President.


 4.7 The trustees shall maintain proper accounts. The accounts of the Trust shall be annually audited by Chartered accountants appointed by the "Board of Trustees".


 4.8 The trust may receive fees by the institutions or school run by trust, whether Technical, Medical or Homeopathic institutes etc.

 

4.9 The trustees shall not be benefited to receive any salary or other remuneration for their services shall be entitled to be recouped out of the trust property or income thereof for all out of pocket expenses actually incurred by them respectively in performing their duties as trustee including all reasonable traveling and hotel expenses including pending meetings of the trustees or incurred in connection with going on of the objects of the trust.


 4.10 The trustees shall have power to accumulate for a period as may be permissible in law, the whole or any part/s of the income of the trust property as the trustees man in their discretion think fit by investing the same and the resulting income there of in any of the investments authorized by law including the provisions of the Income Tax Act , 1961 or any replacement, re-enactment or modification thereof for the time being in force with power to vary such investments for the time being representing the same shall be held for the purpose of this trust and the powers and provisions as are under this deed applicable to the corpus of the trust property shall also apply to the same and treated as one fund.

 

4.11 All moneys forming part of the trust property and requiring investment shall be invested in or upon any of the securities authorized by law investment of trust property;


 4.12 The trustees shall have power to make donations or contributions to any trust, public fund or institution set up for purposes which have similar objects as this trust as approved by the Managing trustees.


 4.13 The receipt in writing of the trustees on behalf of the trust or of Vice-President, Secretary/ Treasurer or authorized representative for any interest, dividend or income or any deed, paper, writing, documents or other moneys and effects payable or deliverable to the trust shall be sufficient and effectual discharge for the same respectively or for so much thereof respectively as in such receipts shall be expressed or acknowledged to be or have been received and the persons to whom or in whose favour the same shall be given his, her and their executors or administrators shall not afterwards be in any obliged concerned to see to the applications of the moneys, property and institutions therein mentioned and acknowledge to be received recoverable or accountable for the loss, misapplication or non-approved thereof.
